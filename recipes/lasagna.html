Lasagna

Description:

It was a crisp fall morning, and.... Just kidding. Here's a recipe for my favorite lasagna.

Ingredients list:

  • 1 lb. Ground Beef
  • 1 large white onion
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 tbsp. Tomato Paste
  • 1 14 oz. can of tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 16 large Lasagna noodles (Homemade preferred)
  • 6 oz. Parmesan cheese (Freshly grated preferred)
  • 4 cups mozarella cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp each of the following:
    • Parsley
    • Basil
    • Salt
    • Italian Seasoning
    • Fennel seeds
    • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Finely dice both the onion and garlic
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef and half of all the desired seasonings, mix until thoroughly incorporated
  3. In a large skillet on medium-high heat, add 1 tbsp of olive oil. Once preheated, add half of the diced onions.
  4. Cook for 6-8 minutes, or until the onions turn translucent and become soft, then add half of the diced garlic and cook an additional 1-2 minutes
  5. Once the onions and garlic are fragrant and slightly browned, add the beef mixture in a large skillet until thoroughly cooked and brown bits (fond) start sticking to the bottom of the pan.
  6. Add your 2 tbsp of tomato paste and cook for 30-60 seconds
  7. Just before the tomato paste begins to burn, add in the entire can of your tomato puree and scrape up all of the fond off the bottom of the pan
  8. Reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 20-30 minutes
  9. While the sauce is simmering, start a large pot of boiling water on the largest burner you have.
  10. Once the water is at a rolling boil, add 2 tbsp of salt (optional) just before adding your lasagna noodles and give them a quick stir.
  11. Cook at a rolling boil for 6-8 minutes, or until al dente (the noodles should be soft on the outside, but slightly undercooked on the inside. The noodles will finish cooking in the oven)
  12. Strain the noodles, and right about this time the sauce should be done cooking.
  13. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  14. In a 9 in. by 12 in. oven-safe dish, add a ladle full of sauce to prevent the noodles from sticking.
  15. Alternate layers of sauce, noodles, and ricotta cheese up until there's approximately 1/2 an inch left at the top of the dish.
    • Make sure to get the fillings all the way to the edge for even cooking!!
  16. If you don't have pre-grated mozarella cheese, grate it now, and then spread the grated cheese evenly across the top of the dish.
  17. Cover the dish tightly with aliminum foil, and bake for 35-45 minutes. 10 minutes before it's done cooking, remove the foil to get some nice golden-brown spots on top of the cheese.
  18. Remove from the oven, and let rest at least 15 minutes before cutting and serving.
